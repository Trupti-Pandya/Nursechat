**4. Emergency Procedures**

- **Heart Attacks & Trauma Cases** – Immediate stabilization in the **Emergency & Trauma Ward** before transferring to specialized units.
- **Stroke Cases** – Fast-track admission to the **Neurology Ward**.
- **Severe Burns** – Direct treatment at the **Burns Unit**.
- **Pregnancy Complications** – Immediate care at the **Maternity Ward**.

**5. Pharmacy & Medication Services**

- **Main Pharmacy** – Ground Floor, Block A | Open 24/7
- **Emergency Prescriptions** – Available through the Emergency Ward
- **Outpatient Prescriptions** – 8 AM - 8 PM daily

**6. Referral and Partner Hospitals**

In cases where we lack certain facilities, we have strong referral partnerships:

- **NYC Royal Infirmary** – For highly specialized surgeries and transplant services.

- **Nottingham City Hospital** – For advanced cancer treatments.

- **Birmingham Children’s Hospital** – For complex paediatric cases.

- **Sheffield Neurology Centre** – For high-risk brain surgeries and neurology cases.

- **London Heart Institute** – For advanced cardiac procedures.

**7. Directional Details**

- **Main Entrance**: Located on NYC Road, accessible via Gate 1.

- **Reception & Emergency**: Ground Floor, Block C, straight from Main Entrance.

- **Wards**: Blocks A to E, floors 1 to 5, with elevators at each block.

- **Labs**: Primarily on 1st and 2nd floors, near respective specialty wards.

- **Doctor Offices**: Adjacent to their respective departments.

- **Parking**: Visitor parking at Gate 2; Staff parking at Gate 3.

Our hospital ensures that every patient gets the best possible care, whether within our hospital or through our trusted medical partners.
